2021 advice

1. Stop worrying so much about the small, meaningless stresses in life and try to remember that we all have the same fate after it all so do whatever you can with the time that makes it feel worth it.

2. Spend some time alone amongst nature, for me thats surfing.

3. If you had a bad day, let it go.

4. Eat the cake. Zero guilt.

5. Travel if it’s your desire, don’t if it’s not.

6. Cherish this moment - bad or good…

7. Talk to your friends. Put down your phone. Are they doing okay?

8. Don’t feel pressured to do what other people might think is a fulfilling life. you might want a mediocre life and that is so okay.

9. Take care of your mental health, it’s as or more important than your physical health.

10. Delete tik tok - Danielle

11. Rest is productive - Michelle
